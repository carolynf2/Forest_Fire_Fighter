Forest Fire Fighter

How to Play:

  • Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your aircraft
  • Press SPACE to drop water and extinguish fires
  • Fly over blue water sources to refill your water tank
  • Press T to plant trees in cleared areas
  • Prevent fires from spreading and restore the forest!

Objective: Extinguish all fires and plant trees to restore the forest.

Warning: Don't let too many trees burn down or you'll lose!
